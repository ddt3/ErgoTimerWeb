## Privacy Statement for ErgoTimer

**Last updated: March 25th 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This privacy statement explains how ErgoTimer ("the app") handles your data. ErgoTimer is a scheduling app designed to help people with Acquired Brain Injury (ABI) and similar conditions plan a balanced working day by alternating mental tasks, physical activities, and rest periods.

We take your privacy seriously. ErgoTimer is designed to work entirely on your device, with no data ever leaving it.

## 2. Data We Collect

ErgoTimer does **not** collect any personal information. The app does not ask for your name, email address, contact details, location, or any other identifying information.

The only data the app stores is your **configuration preferences**, which are saved locally on your device using Android's standard app storage. These preferences include:

- Total working hours per day
- Maximum duration of a mental task
- Minimum duration of a physical task
- Work start time
- Optional bike ride durations (before and after the working day)
- Lunch start and end times
- Micro-pause reminder interval
- Alarm sound preference
- User interface preferences (e.g. calm mode, language)

This configuration data exists solely to restore your settings the next time you open the app.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your configuration preferences are used only to:

- Generate your personalised daily schedule within the app
- Restore your settings when you reopen the app

Your data is never used for profiling, advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

## 4. Data Sharing

ErgoTimer does **not** share any data with third parties. The app has no connection to any external server, cloud service, or third-party service. All processing happens entirely on your device.

## 5. Analytics and Tracking

ErgoTimer does **not** use any analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking tools. There are no third-party SDKs in the app that collect or transmit data.

## 6. Device Permissions

ErgoTimer requests the following Android permissions, which are necessary for core functionality:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` | Schedules accurate alarms for task transitions (Android 12) |
| `USE_EXACT_ALARM` | Schedules accurate alarms for task transitions (Android 13 and later) |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | Reschedules notifications after the device restarts |
| Notifications | Displays task countdown and alerts in the notification area |

None of these permissions are used to access personal data.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

All data stored by ErgoTimer remains on your device for as long as the app is installed. When you uninstall ErgoTimer, all stored preferences are permanently deleted from your device as part of the standard Android uninstall process.

You can also clear the app's data at any time through Android's app settings (Settings → Apps → ErgoTimer → Clear data).

## 8. Children's Privacy

ErgoTimer does not knowingly collect data from children. Because the app collects no personal information at all, it is safe to use regardless of age.

## 9. Security

Because ErgoTimer stores only non-sensitive configuration data locally on your device and never transmits any data, the privacy risks associated with the app are minimal. The app relies on Android's built-in app sandboxing to protect your stored preferences from other apps.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Statement

We may update this privacy statement from time to time. Any changes will be published on the same page where this statement is hosted. The date at the top of this document will always reflect when it was last updated. Continued use of the app after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated statement.
